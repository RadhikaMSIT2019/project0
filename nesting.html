Rules and regulations Some of the rules were changed for the 2020 edition of the IPL.

  • Players will not be allowed to use saliva to shine the ball.
  • All the matches will be played without an audience.
  • Captains will not shake hands after the toss.
  • Front foot no-balls will be adjudicated by the third umpire rather than the on-field umpire.
  • Teams will be allowed replacements if a player tests positive for COVID-19..

COVID-19 The full revised schedule for the tournament was due to be published on 20 August 2020, but was delayed because of COVID-19-related travel restrictions in the UAE.
